1. **Run as Administrator** (recommended for full functionality)
2. Select your **Monitor** from the dropdown
3. Choose **Aspect Ratio** (e.g., `4:3` for classic stretch, or `All` to see everything)
4. Pick your desired **Resolution** (e.g., `1280×1024`)
5. Select **Refresh Rate**
6. Click  **Apply Stretch** to change display resolution
7. Scroll down to **Game Profiles** section
8. Click **Patch Config** on your game to update its config files
9. Click **Launch** to start the game, or launch it manually
10. When done gaming, click **Restore Native** or close the app
